    Bachelor of Business Administration in International Travel and Tourism Management 

    The Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) in International Travel & Tourism Management with IATA Certification is designed to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of the travel and tourism industry, combined with the practical skills and knowledge required to excel in this dynamic field. The course integrates academic learning with hands-on experiences and industry certifications, including the International Air Transport Association (IATA) Foundation certification, to prepare students for successful careers in various sectors of the travel and tourism industry.

    Duration 4 Years
    Eligibility 10+2
    Format Innovative Evaluation
    Commencement Date July 2023

    Frequently Asked Questions

    • 1. What is BBA ITTM?

      Answer - BBA ITTM stands for Bachelor of Business Administration in International Travel & Tourism Management. It's a comprehensive degree program that focuses on providing students with a strong foundation in business administration while specializing in the International travel and tourism industry.

    • 2. Why should I choose BBA ITTM over a traditional BBA program?

      Answer - BBA ITTM offers a unique blend of business management principles and specialized knowledge in the travel and tourism sector. You'll gain insights into global tourism trends, sustainable practices, and acquire the esteemed IATA certification, setting you apart in a competitive job market.

    • 3. What is the IATA certification and how does it benefit me?

      Answer - The IATA certification is offered by the International Air Transport Association, Canada and is globally recognized within the aviation and travel & tourism industry. It provides you with expertise in airline and travel agency operations, enhancing your employability and credibility.

    • 4.What kind of career opportunities can I expect after completing BBA ITTM?

      Answer - Graduates can explore various roles such as Travel Manager, Tourism Marketing Specialist, Destination Manager, Tour Operator, Airline Customer Service Manager, Event Planner, and more. The IATA certification opens doors to aviation-related positions.

    • 5. Is hands-on experience included in the program?

      Answer - Absolutely! We emphasize experiential learning through real-world projects, case studies, internships, and simulated exercises. This practical exposure ensures you're well-prepared to tackle the challenges of the industry.

    • 6. How is technology integrated into the curriculum?

      Answer - The candidates must have completed their 10+2 education (or equivalent) frTechnology plays a crucial role in modern travel and tourism. Our program covers e-tourism, (GDS training (online booking) systems, and digital marketing strategies, giving you the digital prowess needed to thrive in today's landscape.

    • 7. Can I network with industry professionals during the program?

      Answer - Definitely! Our strong industry partnerships provide opportunities for guest lectures, workshops, and networking events. You'll connect with professionals who can offer insights, guidance, and potentially open doors to internships and job placements.

    • 8. Is this program suitable for those interested in international opportunities?

      Answer - Absolutely. BBA ITTM offers a global perspective, preparing you for an international career. You'll gain insights into cross-cultural dynamics, international market trends, and the intricacies of managing businesses across borders.

    • 9. What sets your BBA ITTM program apart from others?

      Answer - Our program combines business acumen with in-depth International travel and tourism knowledge. The IATA certification, practical learning emphasis, and an extensive industry network provide a unique learning experience that equips you for success in the industry.

    • 10.How do I apply for the BBA ITTM program?

      Answer - You can find detailed application instructions on our website. Make sure to check the admission requirements and deadlines. If you have further questions, don't hesitate to contact our admissions team – we're here to guide you through the process!

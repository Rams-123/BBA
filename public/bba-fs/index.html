    Frequently Asked Questions

    • 1. What is ISU degree?

      Answer - ITM Skills University emphasizes preparing executives or working professionals for everyday business challenges and developing insights and knowledge for strategic decision-making and career advancement..

    • 2. Is ITM SU part of ITM Institute or separate?

      Answer -Yes, ITM SU part of ITM Group of Institutes.

    • 3. Can you brief about the BBA-Financial Services?

      Answer -BBA in Financial Services is an undergraduate programme designed to prepare students for a professional qualification in financial services and provides a solid base in the BFSI domain. It will strengthen student’s base, knowledge and proficiency, meeting needs of the industry. Aligns to NEP-2020, keeping in mind the aspirations of students, cultivating critical thinking, analytical acumen, and an in-depth comprehension of the financial services landscape.

    • 4. What sets this program apart from other finance programs?

      Answer - Our program stands out due to its unique blend of industry-aligned education and experiential learning opportunities. We emphasize practical training, immersive internships with renowned financial institutions, exclusive masterclass sessions by industry experts, and hands-on experience in our Business Simulation Lab. This holistic approach ensures that students are well-prepared for the dynamic and evolving world of finance.

    • 5. Is this program suitable for students without a finance background?

      Answer - Absolutely! While a finance background can be advantageous, our program is designed to accommodate students from diverse academic backgrounds. We provide comprehensive foundational courses to ensure that all students develop a strong understanding of finance principles before moving on to more specialized subjects.

    • 6. Are there opportunities for industry exposure and networking?

      Answer - Absolutely! We prioritize industry-academia interaction to provide students with valuable exposure to the finance industry. We arrange industry visits, guest lectures, panel discussions, and seminars featuring industry professionals. These opportunities allow students to gain insights, build networks, and stay abreast of the latest industry trends.

    • 7.Can I pursue further studies after completing this program?

      Answer -Yes, upon completion of the BBA in Financial Services program, students can choose to pursue advanced degrees such as an BBA / in Finance, Financial Services, FinTech, specialized certifications, or professional qualifications such as CFA (Chartered Financial Analyst) or FRM (Financial Risk Manager). The program provides a strong foundation for further academic and professional development.

      • Provides a solid base in the BFSI domain
      • Innovative pedagogy for better learning
      • Inculcates emerging technology in finance
      • Industry-aligned research projects and Immersive internship programs
      • Exclusive masterclass engagements

      • Holistic career development support and 100% placement assistance

    • 11. What is it like to be a student at your University?

      Answer -With a comprehensive curriculum, innovative pedagogy, integration of emerging technologies, and strong industry connections, we are committed to providing you with the tools, knowledge, and skills you need to thrive in this dynamic field. Our holistic approach also focuses on emotional & social quotient and overall personal development, providing personalized mentoring and access to resources for professional growth.

    • 12. Who will be my Professors?

      Answer - Experienced ( with a rich blend of Academic & industry experience) & qualified faculties will your professors.

    • 13.How What Research Projects and/or Internship Opportunities are inculcated in the Program?

      Answer -In the 1st year, students will go for 2 week NGO internship. Also during the 1st year, they need to submit a mini Research Project. After 2nd year, students will attend full-fledged paid internship cum Research Project. Also, if students opt for 4th year, they will further attend a year-long internship which will be converted into Final Research Project.
